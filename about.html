About the Author

Welcome, brave traveler! I'm Aymane, the mind behind the world you're about to dive into. Having always had an interest for exploring the eldritch, the unknown, and the macabre, it's been a long-time passion project of mine to write stories filled with conflict and mystery that realistically weave together cosmic horror, intricate political machinations, intense medieval warfare and the dark undertones of espionage. Heavily inspired by real-life historical events, ancient myths, the relentless struggle of empire-building and Lovecraftian themes that dig deep into the human psyche, various works such as A Song of Ice and Fire, the stories of Middle-Earth and Dune also played monumental roles throughout my earlier years to bring this to life.

Born and raised in Atlanta, Georgia, I’ve spent years attending concerts, traveling, and studying computer science, all while building out this immersive universe. As a storyteller, my goal is to transport readers into a world of complex characters and lore that challenges the boundaries of morality and power.
