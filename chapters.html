Act I: The Wolf and The Hanged Men

In this first act, tensions rise as long-forgotten secrets begin to emerge from the shadows. The ancient forces of Noctyra stir, and the Reimic Imperium teeters on the edge of war. Characters of various backgrounds throughout the realm must face their deepest fears as the darkness begins to close in.

POV Characters

  • II. Khulan, Daughter of Khamul
  • III. Tycho Santoro

Chapters

POV: Noctyra/Arcturus

Prologue (pg. 5-17)

In the Void, the dark entity Noctyra stirs, assembling her corrupted servants to plunge the mortal world into chaos. As she plots her return, Reiman commander Arcturus stands against the rising storm, unknowingly facing Noctyra’s growing influence. A clash between the forces of darkness and mortal resilience is about to begin.

POV: Aurelian

Chapter I (pg. 19-32)

Many years later as tensions rise in the heart of Reima, Aurelian faces an unexpected revelation that pulls him deeper into his family's web of politics and intrigue. With a looming emergency session in the senate and the shadow of his brother's fate, Aurelian is thrust into a game of power far deadlier than he imagined.

POV: Khulan

Chapter II (pg. 33-42)

Haunted by the destruction of her clan, a lone survivor by the name of Khulan navigates the treacherous marshlands with a newfound ally by her side. When danger strikes, she must rely on instincts she didn’t know she had, as dark forces from the past draw ever closer to her path.

POV: Tycho

Chapter III (pg. 43-54)

In the wake of a deadly ambush, imperial agent Tycho navigates the perils of a shattered mission and a rising insurgency. As he wrestles with past horrors and present threats, the Ocularii's true motives remain unclear, forcing him into a dangerous game of survival and manipulation where failure is not an option.

POV: Ana

Chapter IV (pg. 55-66)

Street thief Ana embarks on a high-stakes heist in the shadowed streets of the imperial capital, only to uncover dangerous secrets and old ghosts. When a deadly confrontation leaves her with more than she bargained for, she must outwit both enemies and allies to survive the night—and confront the past she thought she had escaped.

POV: Kastor

Chapter V (pg. 67-80)

In the depths of the Blackest Eye, sea captain and privateer Kastor faces both fists and fate in a brutal fight that will have lasting consequences. Meanwhile, unsettling alliances form as a mysterious figure presents Kastor with a dangerous offer—one that could change the course of his crew’s future. But with betrayal lurking around every corner, trust becomes a rare commodity.

POV: Clovia

Chapter VI (pg. 68-92)

Haunted by mysterious dreams and fragments of a forgotten past, Clovia seeks forbidden knowledge among her sisterhood of the Sanctuna Magiae. With the aid of her friend Laetoria, she risks everything to obtain the elusive elixir known as Aetheris. But as she descends into the shadows, Clovia finds herself facing a perilous unknown that threatens to consume her entirely.

POV: Aurelian

Chapter VII (pg. 93-99)

Aurelian returns to the villa after a turbulent Senate victory, only to find a mysterious symbol hidden in his family’s ancient texts. Shadows of the past begin to stir, and the lines between myth and reality blur as a dark prophecy looms.

POV: Khulan

Chapter VIII (pg. 99-108)

Fleeing the ruins of her home, Khulan finds herself tracking a mysterious caravan deep into the frozen wilderness. As an ambush unfolds, she is forced into an unexpected alliance with a fierce, chained girl. The clash reveals a deadly secret and the rise of a rebel force—the Krovniki.

POV: Tycho

Chapter IX (pg. 109-115)

Tycho's mission in Reima becomes ever more treacherous as he meets with a war-hardened veteran to uncover the depths of corruption and rebellion stirring within the imperium. As new alliances and targets emerge, the stakes rise, and Tycho finds himself facing impossible choices that could spark a civil war. With a volatile assassin at his side and an old soldier full of dark secrets, he must navigate a web of lies where loyalty is just as dangerous as treachery.

POV: Ana

Chapter X (pg. 116-130)

As Ana navigates the aftermath of her daring heist, the threat of danger tightens around her. Drawn deeper into the schemes of Vane, the ruthless Blackleg leader, she must play a deadly game of trust and betrayal. With old debts resurfacing and new enemies closing in, Ana is forced to confront the dark ties of her past and the looming threats in Reima's criminal underworld.

POV: Kastor

Chapter XI (pg. 131-143)

Kastor and the crew of the Messina set sail from Reima under tense skies, navigating through imperial patrols and a growing storm. With the enigmatic augur Pseudolus on board and a mission from the Imperial Trading Company, Kastor can't shake the feeling of unease. As they push eastward, whispers of betrayal and the dangers of the Symphorian Route weigh heavily on him, but nothing prepares them for the monstrous threat lurking in the depths ahead.

POV: Clovia

Chapter XII (pg. 144-154)

Clovia delves into the depths of the void, seeking answers but instead encountering cryptic visions of power, betrayal, and a haunting presence she cannot explain. As the boundary between dream and reality blurs, she must confront a dangerous truth buried deep within the imperium, one that could unravel everything she knows.
