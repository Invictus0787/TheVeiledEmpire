The World of Valara

Explore the vast and mysterious lands of Valara, a realm on the brink of war and supernatural upheaval. From the cold northern expanses to the imperial heartlands and the scorching deserts of the south, every location holds untold stories and dangerous secrets.

Aetheris

Aetheris is the invisible current that pulses through the veins of Valara, binding the mortal realm to the arcane. It is neither light nor shadow, neither divine nor demonic, but something older, more primal—a force that defies the boundaries of life and death. Feared by many, forbidden by most, Aetheris is not a power that is simply learned; it must be awakened, and only a chosen few ever hear its call.

Factions of Valara

The Reimic Imperium

The vast and domineering power of the Reimic Imperium is built upon conquest, discipline, and the might of its legions. Known as the Sunlit Empire, it spans the breadth of the known world, its citizens bound together by a belief in the divine right of their Imperators. Its capital, Reima, glimmers like a jewel amidst its vast territories, yet behind its illustrious facade lies decadence and political infighting. The Imperium's obsession with order, control, and expansion has driven it to the brink of ruin, but its legions, forged in the fires of countless wars, still stand as an indomitable force. The Imperium claims divine favor, believing its rule to be a mandate from the gods themselves, yet whispers abound of dark pacts and eldritch influences guiding the Imperator’s decisions.

The Principalities of Reuvda

Nestled within the dense forests and snow-capped peaks of the far north, the Reuvdan Principalities are a coalition of fiercely independent realms bound together by ancient bloodlines and shared oaths. Though nominally a protectorate of the Imperium, they chafe beneath its rule. The Reuvdan people are known for their fierce loyalty, resilience, and a deep-rooted connection to their land and ancient traditions. However, years of Imperial oppression have stoked the fires of rebellion, and the Krovniki—an insurgent group born from the shadow of the principalities—leads a growing resistance. Though fractured and often at odds with one another, the Reuvdan nobles stand united in their defiance against any force that dares to subjugate their land.

The Krovniki Insurgency

A guerrilla force rising from the oppressed corners of the Reuvdan Principalities, the Krovniki are more than mere insurgents—they are a symbol of the northern people's will to resist Imperial domination. Hardened by years of warfare, survival in the wilderness, and clandestine operations, the Krovniki use hit-and-run tactics, ambushes, and sabotage to undermine the Imperium. Led by the enigmatic figure known as “The Vulture,” their cause has spread like wildfire, attracting outcasts, deserters, and disillusioned nobles. What began as a fight for freedom has now turned into a desperate struggle for survival, fueled by the haunting knowledge that the Imperium will show no mercy in its quest for absolute control.

The Ocularii Agency

Shrouded in secrecy, the Ocularii serve as the Imperium's unseen hand—spies, assassins, and agents tasked with rooting out heresy, sedition, and rebellion. Their influence stretches across borders, manipulating events from the shadows, leaving no trace of their involvement. Whispers speak of the Ocularii’s connections to forbidden magics and ancient relics long thought lost to time. Their methods are ruthless, their loyalty unquestionable. To cross the Ocularii is to invite death, or worse, to be made into one of their many puppets. They are the Imperium’s first and last line of defense against the chaos that threatens to undo its carefully constructed order.

The Akhutai Hordes

A nomadic and fearsome race of warrior clans, the Akhutai sweep across the eastern steppes like a relentless storm. Their culture values strength above all, and their society revolves around constant raiding, war, and conquest. Bound by no empire and answering only to the whims of their warlords, the Akhutai are driven by a bloodlust for battle, their howls echoing across the moors as they descend upon their foes. The Imperium sees them as a barbaric plague, an unpredictable force of destruction that refuses to be tamed. Yet, the Akhutai are more than savage raiders—they are survivors, their way of life honed by centuries of conflict and hardship, their people united by unshakable bonds of blood and honor.

The Sanctuna Magiae

A sisterhood of powerful sorceresses, the Sanctuna Magiae hold the secrets of the arcane in their veiled hands. Once revered as the guardians of forbidden knowledge, they now walk a dangerous line between serving the Imperium and protecting their own ancient order. Cloistered within their hidden temples, they are both feared and sought after for their ability to wield the mysterious force known as Aetheris. Their members engage in intricate rituals, divinations, and alchemical practices to uncover the mysteries of the universe. Yet, as the forces of darkness rise, even the Sanctuna find themselves caught between their duty to the Imperium and the temptation to harness powers far beyond mortal comprehension.

The Imperial Trading Company

The Company is far more than just a merchant enterprise—it is the financial lifeblood of the Reimic Imperium and its sprawling territories. With deep coffers and a vast fleet of ships, the Company controls the flow of goods, gold, and influence across the known world. From the towering palaces of Reima to the far-flung frontier outposts, its reach extends into every corner of the empire and beyond. Officially, the Company serves the Imperium, acting as its economic arm to enrich the state and expand its dominance through trade. Unofficially, it operates like an empire within an empire, wielding more power than many nobles and with ambitions that stretch well beyond commerce.

The Shadow-Born

Once great warriors of bygone eras, they were corrupted by the eldritch entity Noctyra. Now, they serve her as demonic warriors. The Shadow-Born are the secret hand behind many of the dark events plaguing Valara.

Key Characters

Aurelian Opiter

The youngest son of a noble house in Reima, Aurelian finds himself in the midst of a political storm after the mysterious assassination of his father. Struggling with loyalty, duty, and a rising darkness, Aurelian must navigate a dangerous world.

Khulan

A fierce warrior from the Akhutai tribes, Khulan seeks to avenge her clan's destruction by an unknown enemy. Haunted by her past, she must confront forces far greater than she ever imagined.

Tycho Santoro

An imperial agent of the Ocularii, Tycho operates in the shadows, unraveling plots and maintaining the empire’s grip on power. Ruthless and cunning, he walks a thin line between servitude and rebellion, harboring secrets that could undo the Imperium.

Ana Roten

A young thief in the shadowed streets of Reima, Ana’s daring heists and mysterious identity have quickly made her a legend. Driven by more than greed, she seeks to dismantle the corrupt forces ruling the empire while escaping the ghosts of her past.

Kastor Lycyrgos

A sea captain and former noble, Kastor leads a band of privateers while pursuing his own vendettas. Torn between his code of honor and his thirst for revenge, he must decide whether to embrace his fate or fight for his lost homeland.

Clovia

A novice in the Sisterhood of the Sanctuna Magiae, Clovia is haunted by visions of a forgotten past. As she struggles to unlock her powers and uncover the truth behind her lost memories, she risks being consumed by the darkness she seeks to control.

Noctyra

An ancient, malevolent entity of the Void, Noctyra seeks to corrupt the mortal world and ascend to godhood. Her whispers weave through the minds of the powerful, bending them to her will, while her demonic servants pave the way for her return.

Imperator Arcturus

The unyielding ruler of the Reimic Imperium, Imperator Arcturus is both feared and respected for his iron will and tactical brilliance. Bound by duty to maintain the empire's dominance, he struggles to balance internal strife and the looming darkness threatening to tear the realm apart.

Consul Militarum Henricus

A veteran commander of the Reimic Legions, Henricus is a master tactician and Arcturus' most trusted general. Devoted to the Imperium, he faces the growing unrest with a calculating mind but is haunted by the brutal decisions required to preserve order.

Vane

The ruthless leader of the notorious Blacklegs, Vane is a cunning crime lord operating in the shadows of Reima. His complex history with Ana intertwines betrayal and survival, and as he builds his empire from the ground up, Vane's influence stretches far beyond the streets, weaving into the very heart of the imperium’s corruption.
