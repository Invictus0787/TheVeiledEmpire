Story Arcs

Arc I: The Wolf and The Hanged Men

This arc introduces the key players, sets the stage for the central conflict, and hints at the dark forces looming over the empire. Release Date: December 2024

Arc II: The Vulture Hunt

Amid the political turmoil, hidden factions emerge, and ancient enemies return. The players must navigate a web of alliances and betrayals. Release Date: June 2025

Arc III: Reuvda In Flames

The final confrontation between the Reiman Legions and the Krovniki Insurgency unfolds. As loyalties are shattered and lives are sacrificed, the fate of the imperium hangs in the balance. Expected Release: Q4 2025

Planned Sequels

Book II: The Raven's Runes

In the frozen north of Nordenheim, the restless sea stirs with the rise of viking warlords and bloodthirsty raiders. As ancient runes foretell of a conflict yet to come, the northern wastes beckon, where the cold steel of the north will clash with the might of the imperium—and only the gods will decide who stands victorious.

Book III: The Forgotten Prophet

Beneath the blistering sun of Javasfar, the shifting sands whisper of a forgotten prophecy. As the Sultanate verges on the edge of collapse, the Imperial Trading Company weaves its influence, but beyond the Dune Seas, a figure emerges—one who might unite the desert tribes under a single banner, igniting a fire that could burn the empire itself.

Book IV: The Red Horde

Far beyond the Shield Wall, winds of war begin to howl. Tales of a long-forgotten terror echo through the east—the Akhutai ride once more. As the imperium braces itself for an invasion thought to be legend, the tides of destiny surge, and a storm of blood and conquest begins to gather on the horizon.

Book V: The Imperator's Heir

From the shadows of forgotten realms, Noctyra returns with the ancient power of the Void at her command. As darkness creeps across Valara, spanning continents and realms, an empire is thrown into chaos. The imperator’s lineage trembles as the gods and mortals alike brace for a reckoning that will reshape the world.
